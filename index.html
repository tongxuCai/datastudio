Frida Cai's Data Journalism projects

This page lists all my data studio projects from 2019 to 2020 at the Columbia Journalism School.

This is my very first data studio project, in which I analyze the animal bites in Kentucky. I used Python to analyze the dataset and to generate the graphics.
My second data studio project. With AidData's dataset, I analyze where China pours its foriegn investment into. Additional Python languaged used here is Seaborn. I also used Illustrator to visualize the data.
My third data studio project, in which I kept digging into AidData's dataset, attempting to compare China's aid versus non-aid projects overseas, in terms of money flows and geolocations.
In this data computation project, I used one story from ProPublica and made the information more concise and responsive on different templates. Tools used are ai2html and Illustrator.
